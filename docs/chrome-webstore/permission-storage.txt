The extension needs "storage" to prevent duplicate tabs. chrome.storage.sync saves: enabled/disabled; duplicate action; all-windows vs current-window scope; ignore URL parameters; exception URLs/domains; site-specific rules; and the local duplicates-prevented count in the popup. chrome.storage.local stores only optional review-prompt timing (dismiss/snooze). Settings stay on the device via Chrome sync; nothing is sent to our servers. Without storage, rules and exceptions could not be remembered.
